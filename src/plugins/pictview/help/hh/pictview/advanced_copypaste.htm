Working with Clipboard

You can copy entire image or its selected part to the clipboard. You can also replace the image opened in the viewer by the image from the clipboard.

To copy image to clipboard:

Viewer menu:Edit/Copy
Viewer shortcut key:Ctrl+C or Ctrl+Insert
Viewer context menu:Right-click image and choose Copy

To copy selected part of image to clipboard:

  1. Select a part of the image. See how to do it in Using Mouse in Viewer.
  2. Copy selected part of the image to the clipboard:
    Viewer menu:Edit/Copy
    Viewer shortcut key:Ctrl+C or Ctrl+Insert
    Viewer context menu:Right-click image and choose Copy

To paste image from clipboard to viewer:

Viewer menu:Edit/Paste
Viewer shortcut key:Ctrl+V or Shift+Insert
Viewer context menu:Right-click image and choose Paste

Rotating and Flipping Image

You may need to rotate or flip the image in the viewer to enjoy it in the proper orientation. PictView supports these commands:

  • Rotate Left (the L key): rotate the image counterclockwise by a quarter-turn,
  • Rotate Right (the R key): rotate the image clockwise by a quarter-turn,
  • Rotate 180° (the T key): rotate the image by a half-turn,
  • Flip Horizontal (the H key): flip the image horizontally, along the vertical axis,
  • Flip Vertical (the V key): flip the image vertically, along the horizontal axis.

You can find all described commands in viewer menu Edit.

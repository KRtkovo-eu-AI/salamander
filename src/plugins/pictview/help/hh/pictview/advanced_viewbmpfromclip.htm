Viewing Bitmap from Clipboard

Raster images (bitmaps) copied to the clipboard can be opened from there in the viewer. You can then work with such image in the viewer, e.g. save it to disk or even copy any part of it back to the clipboard.

To view bitmap from clipboard:

Menu:Plugins/PictView/View Bitmap from Clipboard
Shortcut key:Ctrl+Shift+B

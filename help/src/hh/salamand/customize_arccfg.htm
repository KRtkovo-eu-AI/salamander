Archivers Autoconfiguration

You can let Open Salamander to find external archivers on your disks.

To configure external archivers:

  1. Open the Archivers Autoconfiguration dialog box: open the Options menu and choose Archivers Autoconfiguration.
  2. Choose the Start button.
  3. Wait until searching is finished.
  4. In the Available archivers box, check the archivers you want to add to the configuration.
  5. Choose the OK button.

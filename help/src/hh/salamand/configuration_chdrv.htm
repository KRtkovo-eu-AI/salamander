Open Salamander: Dialog Boxes: Configuration

Change Drive Menu and Drive Bar

Customize the Change Drive Menu and the Drive Bar on this page.

Dialog Box Options

Select drives that should be visible
Selected drives will be visible in the Change Drive menu and the Drive bar. Click on drive letter to change its selection. You can also use arrow keys to move in drive letters and the Space key to change focused drive letter selection.
Select drives that should be followed by separator
Selected drives will be followed by separator in the Change Drive menu and the Drive bar. Click on drive letter to change its selection. You can also use arrow keys to move in drive letters and the Space key to change focused drive letter selection.
Documents
When checked, the Documents item will be visible in the Change Drive menu and the Drive bar.
Cloud Storage Services
When checked, items for installed cloud storage services will be visible in the Change Drive menu and the Drive bar. Use these items to access local folders of these services. Currently supported services are Google Drive, Dropbox, and Microsoft OneDrive.
Network
When checked, the Network item will be visible in the Change Drive menu and the Drive bar.
Note: if this check box is disabled, the Network item is replaced by the Network plugin (to change visibility of its FS item go to Plugins Manager).
As Other Panel
When checked, the As Other Panel item will be visible in the Change Drive menu. Note: this item does not appear in the Drive bar.

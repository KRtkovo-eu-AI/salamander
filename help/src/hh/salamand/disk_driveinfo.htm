Open Salamander: Working with Disks

Displaying Drive Information

You can obtain information about drive such as used and free space, file system type, disk geometry, and serial number.

To display drive information

  1. Open the drive you want to display information for in the panel.
  2. Open the Drive Information dialog box:
    Menu:Commands/Drive Information...
    Shortcut key:Ctrl+F1
    Mouse:Click the Size item in the Directory Line.
    Toolbar:Choose the Drive Information button on the Top, Middle, or Bottom toolbar.

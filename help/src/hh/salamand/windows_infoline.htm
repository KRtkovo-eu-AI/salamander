Information Line

Information line is a special area at the bottom of the panel, that displays information about the focused item or selected items.

You can:
  • Customize displayed information in Options/Configuration/Appearance/Content of Information line.
  • Click some information to copy this text to the clipboard.
  • Drag some information to copy this text to the Command line.

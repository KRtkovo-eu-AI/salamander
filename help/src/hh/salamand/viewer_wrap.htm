Open Salamander: Using Internal Viewer

Text Wrapping

The viewer can display long text lines as single lines (you need to scroll to the right and to the left to read such long lines) or as more lines (long line is wrapped into several lines to avoid horizontal scrolling).

To turn text wrapping on/off:

Viewer menu:View/Wrap
Viewer shortcut key:Ctrl+W

Open Salamander: Working with Windows and Panels

Activating Panel

There are two panels in Open Salamander. One of them is always active. The active panel is highlighted and receives keyboard input. If you want to know how the active panel looks like, see a screenshot with description in chapter Main Window Components.

To activate other (inactive) panel use the Tab key.

You can activate panel also using a mouse. Just click to its working area (see Panel Components for details).

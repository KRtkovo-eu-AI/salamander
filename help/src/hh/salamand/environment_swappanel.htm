Open Salamander: Working with Windows and Panels

Swapping Panels

Swapping panels allows you to swap the contents of the active and inactive panels.

To swap left and right panels:

Menu:Left/Swap or Right/Swap.
Shortcut key:Ctrl+U

With tabbed panels enabled, swapping moves the entire tab collections between sides, including locked tabs, custom colors, and per-tab directory history. The active tab on each side remains focused so keyboard navigation continues to work exactly as before.

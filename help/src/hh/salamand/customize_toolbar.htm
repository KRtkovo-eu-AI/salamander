Customizing Toolbars

Use the Customize Toolbar dialog box to add, remove, and rearrange the buttons on the toolbars. To customize toolbar, open Options menu, select Customize, and choose toolbar you want to customize.

To add a button to the toolbar:

In the Available Toolbar Buttons box, click the button or separator you want to add, and then click Add.

Note: You can add a separator to divide toolbar buttons into groups. A separator is displayed as a line on the toolbar.

To remove a button from the toolbar:

In the Current Toolbar Buttons box, click the button or separator you want to remove, click Remove, and then click Close.

To change the order of the buttons on the toolbar:

In the Current Toolbar Buttons box, click the button or separator you want to move, click Move Up or Move Down until the item is in the appropriate position, and then click Close.

To Restore the default buttons toolbar configuration:

Click Reset, and then click Close.

Open Salamander: Working with Windows and Panels

Maximizing and Restoring Panel

Panel can be maximized (other panel is minimized at the same time) to provide more space for seeing your files and directories. It is especially useful when viewing your images in thumbnails view mode. When panel is maximized, you can simply restore its original size.

To maximize or restore panel:

  • Menu: Left/Maximize/Restore for left panel or Right/Maximize/Restore for right panel.
  • Shortcut key: Ctrl+F11.
  • Use the Maximize/Restore Panel button in the Directory Line.

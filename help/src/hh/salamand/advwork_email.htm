Emailing Files

This command helps you to send files as email attachment.

To email files:

  1. Select files you want to send.
  2. Choose Email command:
    Menu:Files/Email...
    Shortcut key:Ctrl+E
    Toolbar:Choose the Email button on the Top or Middle toolbar.
  3. Choose the Yes button.

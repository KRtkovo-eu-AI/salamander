Open Salamander: Window Components

Split Bar

A main window is divided into two separate viewing areas, which are called left and right panel. The Split bar defines the edge between the panels.

You can:
  • Drag the Split bar to the desired position. Panel widths will be adjusted. Pressing the Escape key cancels the drag mode.
  • Double-click on the Split bar for the same widths of panels.
  • Right-click on the Split bar and then click the desired ratio of panel widths.
